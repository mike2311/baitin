# Risk Management

## Overview

This document identifies and addresses risks throughout the modernization project.

## High Risks

### 1. Data Migration Complexity
- **Risk:** 186 tables, complex relationships
- **Impact:** High - Data loss or corruption
- **Mitigation:** Extensive testing, parallel run, rollback plan

### 2. User Adoption
- **Impact:** High - Users reject new system
- **Mitigation:** Early user involvement, comprehensive training

### 3. Timeline Pressure
- **Impact:** High - 15-month timeline is aggressive
- **Mitigation:** Phased approach, resource allocation

### 4. Technology Stack Issues
- **Impact:** Medium - May need to change stack
- **Mitigation:** Early validation, proof of concept

## Medium Risks

### 1. Scope Creep
- **Impact:** Medium - Timeline delays
- **Mitigation:** Strict scope control, change management

### 2. Resource Availability
- **Impact:** Medium - Need skilled developers
- **Mitigation:** Early recruitment, training

### 3. Performance Issues
- **Impact:** Medium - Poor performance
- **Mitigation:** Performance testing, optimization

## Risk Mitigation Strategies

### General Strategies
- **Early Validation:** Validate approach early (PoC)
- **Phased Delivery:** Reduce risk through phases
- **Comprehensive Testing:** Extensive testing at each phase
- **Stakeholder Communication:** Regular communication

## Summary

Comprehensive risk management covering identification, assessment, and mitigation of project risks.
